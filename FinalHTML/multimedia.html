Maa Inti Bangaram (2026)

Maa Inti Bangaram is an upcoming 2026 Telugu action-drama film starring Samantha Ruth Prabhu as a housewife who turns into a fierce, rebellious figure, marking her comeback to content-driven regional cinema. Directed by B.V. Nandini Reddy, the film promises an intense, raw story exploring the duality of a woman balancing domestic life with a hidden, violent past.

Lokah(2025)

Lokah Chapter 1: Chandra (2025) is a Malayalam-language superhero fantasy thriller directed by Dominic Arun and produced by Wayfarer Films. The film follows Chandra (Kalyani Priyadarshan), a mysterious, goth-style woman who moves to Bengaluru to tackle a dark, supernatural, and corrupt force, rooted in Kerala folklore. Described as a "vampire-superhero" hybrid, she possesses extraordinary abilities and works as a nocturnal courier while navigating a world of organized crime. With a focus on intense action, stylish visual effects, and a haunting background score, the film serves as the opening chapter of a larger, ambitious "Lokah" cinematic universe, often praised for its unique, localized, and female-led approach to the genre.
